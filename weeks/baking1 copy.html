PéPé Bakes - Midweek Treat

Thursday, September 4, 2025
Miso Chocolate Chip Cookies
Charlie + Mya

A rich and chocolatey cookie with a hint of umami and depth from browned butter.

These cookies loosely follow a Butternut Bakery recipe. Browning the butter before mixing in the miso and sugars gives the cookies a deep, caramel-like flavor. We doubled the amount of miso to amp up the umami notes that balances the sweetness. Using a roughly chopped dark chocolate bar lead to a more intersting texture and enjoyable eating experience. This recipe is still delicious when gluten free flour is substituted for regular flour.

Made about 12 cookies for a midweek treat.
